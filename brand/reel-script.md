## Pre-Film Checklist

**Before going to gym:**
- [ ] Screen record the app flow at home (better lighting)
- [ ] Know what equipment the gym has
- [ ] Wear workout clothes
- [ ] Charge phone

**At the gym:**
- [ ] Find spot with decent lighting
- [ ] Film the hook first (Shot 1) ‚Äî confident energy
- [ ] Film workout shots while you have energy (slams, band pushes, lunges, running)
- [ ] Film talking parts last (slightly sweaty = authentic)
- [ ] Get the gym pan shot

---

## Filming Order (most efficient)

1. Screen record app flow at home first
2. Film hook (Shot 1) ‚Äî do multiple takes, pick the best
3. Film running shot
4. Film workout shots: slams ‚Üí band pushes ‚Üí lunges
5. Film closing line (Shot 7)
6. Get the gym pan shot anytime

---

## Editing Tips

1. **Cut tight** ‚Äî remove all pauses/ums
2. **Add captions** ‚Äî most people watch on mute (especially the $4000 hook)
3. **Speed up screen recording** ‚Äî 1.5x or 2x
4. **Quick cuts in montage** ‚Äî 2 seconds max per exercise
5. **Trending sound** ‚Äî low volume under your voice (optional)
6. **End card** ‚Äî "hytrain.pro" text on screen for last 2-3 seconds
7. **Bold text on hook** ‚Äî Make "$4000" pop visually
